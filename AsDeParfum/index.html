La Vie Est Belle Rose Extraordinaire

La vie est belle Rose Extraordinaire is a warm floral perfume that has modernized Lancôme’s iconic rose for an alluring feminine & floral trail, enclosed in a new blooming interpretation of our iconic crystal smile bottle.

La vie est belle Rose Extraordinaire is a tribute to the most iconic flowers of perfumery. The heart of this warm floral perfume is composed of our signature iris and three precious rose extracts: fresh rose water, rose accord, and rose damascena absolue. Its floral heart is enhanced by enveloping woody musk undertones.
